Team of the Week

Scuderia Ferrari

Ferrari's recent form on the race track has earned it the title of Team of The Week. Its driver duo of Carlos Sainz and Charles Leclerc are continuing to put dazzling displays of speed as they battle the Red Bulls for 1st.

Driver of the Week

Alexander Albon

Alex Albon of Williams F1 Racing has earned Driver of the Week due to his abosutely massive performance last weekend. After he pitted in the early stages of the race, he somehow managed to nurse his hard tires for 51 laps. His heroic efforts help earn the team and himself 2 points. Will he keep it up to earn back to back Driver of the Week next week?
